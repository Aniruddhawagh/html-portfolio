The Best Movie According To Aniruddha

My Top 3 Movies Of All-Time.

  1. Tumbaad(2018) :-

    IMDB RATING:- 8.2/10
    (Horror,Drama,Fantasy)

    A mythological story about a goddess who created the entire universe. The plot revolves around the consequences when humans build a temple for her first-born.

  2. Back to the future :-

    IMDB RATING:- 8.5/10
    (Sci-Fi,Comedy,Adventure)

    Marty McFly, a 17-year-old high school student, is accidentally sent 30 years into the past in a time-traveling DeLorean invented by his close friend, the maverick scientist Doc Brown. Awesome sci-fi movie

  3. Kabhi Khushi Kabhi Gam :-

    IMDB RATING:- 7.4/10
    (Drama,Musical,Romance)

    After marrying a poor woman, rich Rahul is disowned by his father and moves to London to build a new life. Years later, his now-grown younger brother Rohan embarks on a mission to bring Rahul back home and reunite the family.
